New England River Flows

Current discharge (CFS) for paddling rivers in MA & CT. Tap a river name for its weekly graph.
RiverFlowStatus

Data sources. Hoosic, Farmington (Rainbow gage), and Still River are pulled live from the USGS Water Services API. The Farmington figure is the sum of the Farmington gage (01186000) and the Still River gage (01186500).

The Upper Deerfield (Fife Brook) and Dryway sections have no public API or historical feed — their sources publish only a single current value. A scheduled GitHub Action scrapes those values every couple of hours and stores them in this repository, so their graphs are a genuine rolling history that fills in over the first week after deployment.
